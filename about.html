About Tuxedo Wellness

Tuxedo Wellness offers a variety of yoga, energy & bodywork, and squash services. Our intention is to help you build the confidence you seek. Self reflection is key for anyone looking to succeed in life. We offer you different building blocks to become the strongest you.

The first step in any self journey is accepting who you are and where are you are currently in your life. Then asking what your goals are and how you want to achieve those goals. Our programs are based on the individuals that take the courses. We can modify for any corporate goal, athletic goal, or mental achievement.

Tuxedo Wellness instructors come from different and unique backgrounds. Get setup with a consultation and see who you connect with.

Shelly Blatt
Yoga Instructor

I became a yoga instructor so I can help others empower themselves. After practicing yoga for over 10 years I realized how wonderful yoga is and wanted to share it with others. My goal is to teach others how to connect the mind to the body and to believe in the power within oneself. My classes will focus on alignment, balance, confidence and learning how to own your breath and your body. I believe the connection to the pose is more important then the perfection of the pose. It truly motivates me to see my students gain confidence in themselves. I received my 200 hour yoga teaching certification from Yoga Mountain, New City, NY in 2011. I received my 500 hour yoga certification from the Karma Vault, Nanuet, NY in 2014. Own your breath. Own your body. Yoga is for any body.

Audrey W
Yoga Instructor

Audrey loves and is dedicated to the practice of yoga. She enjoys being present for the process and taking all the lessons she learns on her mat and bringing them off her mat and out into the world. Inspiration is found in the world around her, the seasons, all living creatures, rock and roll music, poetry,dance, hugs, smiles, human connection, gratitude and deep down love. She truly believes the sweetness and magic of this practice. Audrey has been trained in Vinyasa Flow, Restorative, Prenatal, Trauma and Mommy and Baby Yoga. Her teachers include Lauren Magarelli, Mary Dana Abbott, Jamie Lyn Skolnick, Carrie Parker and Kelly Kamm. When teaching she focuses on mindfulness and feeling. Setting up poses to go deeper into the body, the mind and the breath. Working to create a balance between movement and stillness. Deeply rooted in gratitude.
